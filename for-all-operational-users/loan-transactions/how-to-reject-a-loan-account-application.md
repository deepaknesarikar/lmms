# How to Reject a Loan Account Application

## How to Reject a Loan Account Application



#### **To reject a loan account application** <a href="#howtorejectaloanaccountapplication-torejectaloanaccountapplication" id="howtorejectaloanaccountapplication-torejectaloanaccountapplication"></a>

Locate the loan account by navigating to it through the client or group that holds the loan account.

![](../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot33.png)

1. Select **Reject** from the more.
2. Select the **Rejected on** date from the calendar pop-up.

![](../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot34.png)



1. Type any relevant notes.
2. Click **Submit.**
